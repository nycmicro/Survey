System Overview

Confocal and super-resolution in one system

The W1 SoRa platform combines full-field spinning disk confocal imaging with resolution enhancement designed to deliver fast, specimen-friendly imaging with no special specimen preparation required.

  • Optical Pixel Reassignment based resolution enhancement with no special specimen preparation or computation
  • Approx. 1.4x improvement in resolution using magnification and microlensing, with further improvement up to 2x using deconvolution
  • Easily switch to full-field confocal imaging at 22mm FOV
  • Yokogawa spinning disk technology for specimen-friendly, high-speed confocal imaging
Microscope Platform

Nikon Eclipse Ti2 microscope, optics and software

  • Full integration with Nikon’s flagship inverted microscope platform, the Eclipse Ti2
  • Industry-leading autofocusing system (PFS4) for perfect drift correction
  • Flexible platform for adding additional imaging applications
  • Nikon optics designed for super-resolution applications, including silicone immersion objectives for live cells and deeper imaging
  • NIS-Elements software for acquisition, analysis and visualization
